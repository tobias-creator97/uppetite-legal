Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does ingredient recognition work?

Take a photo of your fridge or your ingredients. Our AI automatically recognizes what's in the photo and suggests matching recipes.

Are my photos stored?

No. Your photos are used solely for real-time ingredient recognition and are not stored afterwards.

What is Uppetite Premium?

With Premium you get up to 5 recipe suggestions per request, detailed nutritional information, advanced time filters, and up to 30 generations per month.

How can I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your Name at the top → Subscriptions → select Uppetite → Cancel Subscription.

I have allergies. Can the app take that into account?

Yes! In your preferences you can set allergies, intolerances, and disliked ingredients. These are strictly respected during every recipe generation.

The app doesn't recognize my ingredients correctly.

Make sure the photo is well-lit and the ingredients are visible. You can manually adjust the ingredient list after recognition.
